## Reasons
 1. The university panel felt the proposed scope may be too small for a one-year industry project. We agreed that the project should still keep a clear MVP, but also include extended features for the full-year delivery.
 2. Real-world requirements gathering is needed to ensure the system reflects actual school communication challenges in Sri Lanka.

## Decisions
 1. Students will think further about additional useful features that can be added beyond the MVP.
 2. The goal is not to add random features, but to identify features that fit the product problem domain and can realistically be delivered.
 3. The team will visit Sri Lankan schools to understand real communication challenges and refine the system requirements.
