# VidyaConnect
Sri Lanka School Communication Platform

## Overview

VidyaConnect is a mobile-first, role-aware school communication platform designed specifically for Sri Lankan schools. The platform aims to replace fragmented communication channels such as WhatsApp groups, printed notices, phone calls, and verbal messages with a structured, secure, and centralized communication system.

VidyaConnect enables school administrators, teachers, parents/guardians, and students to access important school-related information through a single platform while reducing administrative workload and improving communication efficiency.

## Problem Statement

Sri Lankan schools currently rely on fragmented and inefficient communication methods, including WhatsApp groups, printed circulars, phone calls, and verbal messages through students.

These approaches often lead to:

Parents missing important announcements  
Teachers repeatedly sharing the same information through multiple channels  
Administrators lacking delivery and response tracking  
Students missing assignment deadlines  
Paper-based consent forms requiring manual follow-up  

There is currently no centralized platform that provides announcements, assignments, attendance management, consent forms, notifications, and school events in one place.

## Project Objectives

Develop and deploy a functional MVP for school communication and administration.  
Implement structured communication between schools, teachers, parents, and students.  
Digitize attendance and absence management processes.  
Replace paper-based consent forms with digital workflows.  
Provide a unified calendar and event management system.
